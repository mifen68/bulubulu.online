About Monster Survivors

Monster Survivors is an action-packed survival game where your goal is simple yet challenging: stay alive as long as possible against increasingly difficult waves of monsters. Choose your character, collect power-ups, and upgrade your abilities as you battle through hordes of enemies in this addictive game.

With pixel-perfect gameplay, intuitive controls, and a variety of characters to unlock, Monster Survivors offers endless hours of entertainment for gamers of all skill levels. Every playthrough is different, keeping the experience fresh and exciting.

How to Play

Getting started with Monster Survivors is easy:

  • Use WASD or arrow keys to move your character around the map
  • Your character attacks automatically when enemies are in range
  • Collect experience orbs from defeated enemies to level up
  • Choose new abilities or upgrade existing ones when you level up
  • Survive as long as possible and aim for a high score!

The longer you survive, the stronger and more numerous the monsters become. Plan your upgrades wisely to create the most effective build for your playstyle.
